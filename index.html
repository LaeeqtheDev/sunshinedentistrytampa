Meet the doctors

Two sisters.
One chair you'll actually like.

Dr. Daira Avila is a proud University of Florida graduate — Doctorate in Dental Medicine and undergrad, Go Gators. She's Invisalign certified, treats every case as a cosmetic case, and has spent hundreds of hours in continuing education on the latest techniques.

She's joined by her sister, Dr. Daima Avila. Together they've built the kind of practice patients stay with for a decade — the kind where the staff learns your name on the first visit.

When she's not in the chair, Dr. Daira volunteers dental care to underprivileged communities across Florida and the Caribbean.
